The Boat House Bar

The Boat House Bar is located by the canal. It is a great place to relax, enjoy some food and a selection of drinks. The staff is super friendly and the nautical theme decor of this place is fantastic.

Skipton Castle

With its over 900-year history, Skipton Castle is one of the best preserved medieval castles in England. Located at the top of Skipton's High Street, Skipton Castle boasts impressive towers that invite visitors to explore every corner.

Bolton Abbey

The Bolton Abbey estate boasts 30,000 acres of breathtaking beauty. It is great destination for families seeking quality time exploring the riverside, fishing, and savoring the local cuisine. Wheelchair access is available to most amenities.

The Coffee House

Known for its excellent in-house-made food and quality artisan coffee, the Coffee House specializes in quality artisan coffee. In addition to being located near the Skipton market high street, they have hundreds of loyal customers.

Victoria Mill

The Victoria Mill was built for William Wilkinson in 1847, and on the First Edition Ordnance Survey map (1854) it is listed as a corn mill. In addition to producing flour and animal feed, the mill is thought to have supplied breweries with grain as well.
