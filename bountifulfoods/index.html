About Carlsbad

Close to perfection

Known for its world-class resorts and near-perfect weather, Carlsbad has it all.

With an international theme park and an international golf capital, it has been dubbed the golf capital of the world.

Indeed, Carlsbad is a great place to live, with miles of beaches, an extensive variety of housing options, superb shopping experience, and a world-class public library.
