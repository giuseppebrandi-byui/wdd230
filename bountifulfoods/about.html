About Us

A man with a vision

Founded in 1985 by Marco Rossi, Bountiful Foods is one of Carlsbad's largest grocery companies.

Originally from Caserta, Italy, Marco moved to the US when he was 18 years old. Both his father and mother were farmers and taught him the importance of taking care of the family farm. The young man often assisted his father in selling fruits and vegetables, but he had greater plans and wanted to travel.

In Carlsbad, CA, he was invited by his uncle, Giovanni, to assist his grocery business, which he sold to Marco after retiring.

In the meantime, Marco had met his future wife Catherine who helped him with his new business venture

A family tradition

The business was renamed Marco Market by Marco and Catherine, and the two of them worked together to make it successful.

Having been exposed to farmers' culture from his youth, Marco decided to establish business relationships with farmers and supervise the quality of their products.

In order to import exotic fruits and vegetables from South America and the Caribbean, his wife suggested that he talk to farmers there.

Marco and Catherine became well known in Carlsbad for the variety of produce available in their store. Marco Market was just the beginning of their successful business venture.
