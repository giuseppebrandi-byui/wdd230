    • Target Audience

    The target audience is mainly entrepreneurs who might need to build relationships with other business owners, receive specialized guidance or training, organize events, and have a better influence on certain government policies that might affect businesses.

    Members are willing to pay their membership to take advantage of the services the chamber of commerce might provide.

    Personas

    Chris Green

    As a 27-year-old entrepreneur, he wants to establish business relationships with more established companies to expand his business. He is ambitious, has been in business for a few years, and will whatever it takes to be successful.

    Mary Jones

    She is 55 years old, has been in business for 20 years, and is well respected in the community. According to her, the government should do more to support businesses. She believes that the chamber of commerce will advocate, connect, and fight for the growth of local businesses.

    Scenarios

    Scenario 1: Is there a way to network with other businesses outside of our community effectively?

    Scenario 2: Can the chamber of commerce help me with organizing my next event?

    Scenario 3: Would the chamber of commerce be able to influence government policies that currently affect my business?

    Scenario 4: How can I access discounts offers from other members?

    Scenario 5: Is there any educational resource available at my chamber of commerce?
