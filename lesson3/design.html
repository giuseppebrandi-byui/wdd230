Colors & Typography

The main foints used for the Skipton Chamber of Commerce are Ubuntu and DM Sans. Ubuntu is an OpenType font family, designed to be modern and humanist. DM Sans is a low-contrast geometric sans serif design, intended to use in smaller text.

The primary color we will implement in the design is "cucumber" and the accent color will be "golden ginger".
